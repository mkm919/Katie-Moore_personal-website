June Book Recommendations

Title Author Rating Notes
The Raven Boys Maggie Stiefvater My rating is 4 out of 5 This is the first book of a 4 book series. This paranormal tale includes unlikely frienships and magic.
House of Flame and Shadow Sarah J. Maas My rating is 5 out of 5 This is the third installment of the series. This incredible fantasy romance must be read in order, and it will take you to another world where angels, fae, and more exist. This series has a strong female lead, heroing tasks, love, and humor.
Divine Rivals Rebecca Ross My rating is 5 our of 5. This is the first of two books. This enemies-to-lovers fantasy is full of hope, heartbreak, and a strong female lead. You will need the second book ready immediately.
